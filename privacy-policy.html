Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Kick & Bass is a static editorial website. We do not require user accounts, payments, newsletter signups, or login credentials to read the site.

Information We Collect

The site itself does not intentionally collect personal information from readers. Third-Party Links

Articles may link to third-party services such as FIFA, YouTube, Spotify, X, QQ Music, or other music and news sources. Those sites have their own privacy practices. Visiting them is subject to their policies, not this one.

Cookies and Analytics

The current static site does not set advertising cookies or run a first-party analytics system. If analytics, ads, embeds, or comment tools are added later, this policy should be updated before those tools are enabled.

Submitted Content

If readers contact the site owner or submit corrections through external tools, the information provided may be used to respond, verify a correction, or improve site content.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as the site changes. The date at the top of the page will reflect the latest version.

# How to cover official World Cup songs without sounding like a press release

This placeholder defines the editorial standard for official music coverage.

The basic article can be short: what was announced, who is involved, where to listen, and which official source confirms it. The better article adds context: how the song fits the artist's catalog, whether it works in a stadium, how fans are responding, and what remixes or clips are already forming around it.

## Coverage format

- Verified announcement and credits
- Listen link or embed
- One paragraph of music context
- Fan response and platform momentum
- Clear source links

Do not copy lyrics. Do not rehost audio. Use embeds and attribution.

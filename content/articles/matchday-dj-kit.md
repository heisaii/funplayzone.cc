# The five crates every matchday DJ should keep close

This placeholder turns playlist thinking into a practical DJ framework.

A matchday DJ needs more than a hype folder. The useful crates are national classics, city sounds, neutral crowd movers, tension tracks, and post-match release. Each one solves a different emotional problem.

## Five crates

- National classics for identity
- Host-city tracks for place
- Neutral crowd movers for mixed rooms
- Tension tracks for halftime and late drama
- Release tracks for celebration or exit

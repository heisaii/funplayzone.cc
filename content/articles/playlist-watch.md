# A matchday playlist needs three moods, not one endless hype loop

This is a placeholder playlist essay. The final version can embed a Spotify or YouTube playlist and explain the editorial logic behind it.

Start with arrival music: songs for transit, city walks, and the slow approach to the stadium. Move into kickoff pressure with bigger drums, faster hooks, and tracks fans can shout over. After full time, the playlist should split: celebration, recovery, or the long quiet ride back.

## Three useful blocks

- Arrival: city energy, travel rhythm, low-pressure discovery
- Kickoff: chants, drums, hooks, regional classics
- After full time: victory release, heartbreak, late-night reset

Keep the playlist flexible. A noon group-stage match and a knockout night need different temperature.

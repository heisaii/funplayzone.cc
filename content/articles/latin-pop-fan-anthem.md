# The fan anthem test: can a song survive a noisy street corner?

This placeholder is for a recurring fan-anthem column.

Tournament songs work when they can escape polished release strategy. A useful test is simple: can the hook survive bad speakers, traffic, crowd noise, clipped social video, and a group of fans who only know one line?

## The test

- Is the hook singable after one listen?
- Does the rhythm work while walking?
- Can the chorus become a chant?
- Does the clip still make sense without context?

The final column can compare official tracks, fan edits, and regional songs that are moving before major outlets notice.

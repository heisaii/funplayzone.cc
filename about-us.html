About Kick & Bass

Kick & Bass is an independent music and culture blog focused on the sound around World Cup 2026: official songs, fan edits, K-pop and Chinese pop, host-city scenes, playlists, broadcast music, and the smaller rooms where tournament memories are made.

We cover football as entertainment culture, not only as a match schedule. That means listening to radio shows, watching music videos, following artist and fan communities, and paying attention to how songs move through hotels, airports, bars, street markets, watch parties, and short-video platforms.

Editorial Approach

Our articles are written as cultural analysis and music discovery. When a story discusses an artist or a possible World Cup-adjacent sound, it should not be read as a claim of official involvement unless the article clearly cites a confirmed source.

Kick & Bass is not affiliated with FIFA, the FIFA World Cup, X, Spotify, YouTube, QQ Music, or any artist, label, club, venue, or broadcaster mentioned on the site.
